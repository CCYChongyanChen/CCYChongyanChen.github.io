Motivation:

We aim to build a smart system that can automatically answer visual questions from blind people. The images and questions you will see are from visually impaired people and the answer are from crowd workers.


We ask you to:

Carefully review the question, image, and the answer provided, and then finish step 1, step2, and if provided, step 3.
Step 1: indicate if there is more than one question asked.
Step 2: indicate if the answer is referring to more than one region/object.
Step 3: Step 3 will only be activated if you select two "No" for Step 1 and 2.
For step 3, either draw ONE closed polygon to ground region that the answer is referring to by clicking the image. Or You may select "cannot draw" option and indicate the reason why you cannot ground the answer for this image.
